THE MAGIC FLUTE

  • Opera, Family Entertainment, Fantasy Show
  • Saturday, Sept 16, 7.00PM
  • Caudan Arts Centre, Port Louis

Duration: 2h 4min

Price: Rs 300

The Magic Flute by Mozart.

Full of magical melodies and fantastic creatures, Mozart’s Magic Flute is the perfect opera for families. The beloved tale follows Prince Tamino and bird-catcher Papageno on an adventure to rescue Princess Pamina as they face unexpected trials and challenges on their journey. The Mauritian version of Mozart’s masterpiece is set in a fantastic underwater world. It is shortened to about 90 minutes and performed in a mix of English and French. The famous air of the Queen of the Night with its spectacular high staccato notes will not be missing, nor will the very low notes of the priest Sarastro.
